Notice

Policy for Re-Stocking or refusal after payment on new bikes.
Effective Jan. 2 2022

There is a re-stocking fee of 20% for a new bike which was purchased in the store or over the phone IF it is returned.

If the condition is perfect the 15% fee will apply. If there is damage, no return is accepted.

If there is a defective part or frame, issue a full reimbursement will apply or 100% repair will be done if the customer prefers.


Credit card fees will be paid by the customer at time of refund if applicable.
